    • Vacation: Regular time off for rest and recreation
    • Sick Leave: Time off due to illness or medical appointments
    • Emergency: Urgent unforeseen circumstances

    • Official Business: Work-related travel or assignments

    Tips for Leave Requests

    • Submit leave requests as early as possible to ensure proper planning
