    About the System

    The Other Bangsamoro Communities Management System supports the OOBC's mandate of serving Bangsamoro communities outside BARMM through comprehensive data management, needs assessment, and coordination functions.

    Comprehensive data management
    Needs assessment tools
    Multi-stakeholder coordination
    Policy tracking and recommendations
    Monitoring & evaluation of PPAs and OBC requests

    Mission Statement

    To promote the welfare and aspirations of the Other Bangsamoro Communities through policy recommendations and systematic programs guided by Moral Governance.

    "BANGSAMORO KA, SAAN KA MAN!"
