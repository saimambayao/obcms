    OBC Data Collection Guidelines

    Standards and guidelines for collecting and managing Other Bangsamoro Community data.

    Data Quality Standards

    Accuracy Requirements

    • All population figures must be verified through local barangay records
    • Geographic coordinates should be obtained using GPS devices when possible
    • Community names should reflect local usage and be confirmed with residents
    • Contact information must be current and verified within the last 6 months

    Completeness Standards

    • Minimum required fields: Community Name, Barangay, Population Estimate
    • Priority fields: Settlement Type, Primary Ethnolinguistic Group, Development Status
    • Optional but valuable: Demographic breakdown, Access to services, Cultural information

    Collection Process

    1. Preparation Phase

    Coordinate with local LGU officials and barangay captains before conducting field visits.

    2. Field Assessment

    Conduct community visits with local guides and key informants. Use standardized forms for data collection.

    3. Verification

    Cross-reference data with multiple sources including LGU records, DSWD lists, and community leaders.

    4. Documentation

    Enter data into the system within 48 hours of collection. Include source references and verification notes.

    Cultural Sensitivity Guidelines

    Important Considerations

    • Respect Islamic customs and prayer times during visits
    • Use appropriate greetings in local languages when possible
    • Involve local religious and traditional leaders in the process
    • Be sensitive to gender dynamics and family structures
    • Understand the significance of traditional governance systems
