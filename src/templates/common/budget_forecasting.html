    Forecast Methodology

    Budget forecasts are calculated using linear trend analysis based on historical budget allocation data from the past 5 years. The system computes the average year-over-year growth rate and projects future budgets using this growth factor.

    Formula: Projected Budget(year N) = Last Budget × (1 + Growth Rate)^N
